Detailed image specifications

🏢 Profile photo (business logo)

High Priority
Minimum Size: 720×720 pixels
Recommended: 1200×1200 pixels
Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (Square)
File Size: 10KB - 5MB
Format: JPG or PNG
Display: Circular crop on most platforms

💡 Best practices:

  • Use your business logo with a clean, solid background
  • Ensure logo is centered and fills most of the square
  • Avoid text-heavy images as they become unreadable when cropped
  • Test how it looks when cropped to a circle
  • Use high contrast colors for better visibility

🌅 Cover photo (header image)

High Priority
Optimal Size: 1024×575 pixels
Minimum: 480×270 pixels
Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (Landscape)
File Size: 10KB - 5MB
Format: JPG or PNG
Display: Full width header

💡 Best practices:

  • Show your business exterior, interior, or key products/services
  • Use bright, high-quality images that represent your brand
  • Avoid cluttered scenes - keep the focus clear
  • Consider seasonal updates to keep profile fresh
  • Ensure important elements aren't cut off on mobile

🏪 Additional business photos

Medium Priority
Minimum Size: 720×720 pixels
Recommended: 1200×1200 pixels
Aspect Ratio: 1:1 preferred, 4:3 or 3:2 acceptable
File Size: 10KB - 5MB each
Format: JPG or PNG
Quantity: 3-10 photos recommended

📷 Photo categories to include:

🏢 Exterior

Storefront, building, signage

🏠 Interior

Workspace, seating, atmosphere

📦 Products

Items you sell or create

🛠️ Services

Work in progress, before/after

👥 Team

Staff members, owner

⚡ At Work

Action shots, process

📝 Google posts images

Medium Priority
Optimal Size: 1200×900 pixels
Minimum: 400×300 pixels
Aspect Ratio: 4:3 (Landscape) preferred
File Size: 10KB - 5MB
Format: JPG or PNG
Frequency: Weekly posts recommended

📋 Post types & image ideas:

📢 What's New

New products, services, or business updates

🎉 Events

Upcoming events, workshops, or special occasions

💰 Offers

Promotions, discounts, and special deals

📰 News

Company news, awards, achievements

Technical requirements & guidelines

📏 Size limits

  • Minimum: 720×720 pixels for most images
  • Maximum: No upper limit, but 5MB file size limit
  • Cover Photos: 1024×575 pixels optimal
  • Posts: 1200×900 pixels recommended

💾 File requirements

  • Formats: JPG and PNG only
  • File Size: 10KB minimum, 5MB maximum
  • Quality: High resolution preferred
  • Compression: Minimal compression for clarity

🎨 Content guidelines

  • Quality: Professional, well-lit photos
  • Relevance: Directly related to your business
  • Freshness: Recent photos, avoid outdated content
  • Accuracy: Current business appearance

🚫 Content restrictions

  • No watermarks or promotional overlays
  • No blurry or low-quality images
  • No inappropriate or offensive content
  • No copyrighted material you don't own

📈 Image optimisation tips for better performance

🎯

Focus on quality

Use professional, high-resolution photos that clearly represent your business. Poor quality images can negatively impact customer perception and local search rankings.

🌟

Show your best features

Highlight your unique selling points through images. Show clean, organized spaces, quality products, and professional service delivery.

📅

Keep images current

Regularly update photos to reflect current business appearance, seasonal changes, and new products or services. Fresh content improves engagement.

📱

Mobile-first approach

Most users view business profiles on mobile devices. Ensure your images look great on small screens and load quickly.

🏷️

Use descriptive filenames

Name your image files descriptively (e.g., "downtown-cafe-interior.jpg") before uploading to help with SEO and organization.

⚡

Optimise file size

Balance image quality with file size. Compress images to reduce loading times while maintaining visual quality for better user experience.

❌ Common image mistakes to avoid

📐

Wrong aspect ratios

Using landscape images for profile photos or square images for cover photos leads to poor cropping and unprofessional appearance.

✅ Solution: Follow the specific aspect ratio requirements for each image type.
🌙

Poor lighting

Dark, blurry, or poorly lit photos create a negative impression and may not display well across all devices.

✅ Solution: Use natural lighting or professional lighting equipment for clear, bright images.
📝

Too much text in images

Text-heavy images become unreadable when resized or cropped, especially in profile photo circular format.

✅ Solution: Use minimal text and rely on image quality to convey your message.
🏢

Outdated business photos

Old photos showing previous layouts, products, or branding confuse customers and reduce trust.

✅ Solution: Regularly audit and update photos to reflect current business state.
