Frequently Asked Questions


            

                

                    
What is schema markup for local businesses?

                    
Schema markup is structured data that helps search engines understand your business information. For local businesses, it includes details like address, phone number, opening hours, and business type to improve local search visibility and enable rich snippets in search results.

                


                

                    
Why is local business schema important for SEO?

                    
Local business schema helps search engines display rich snippets in search results, improves local search rankings, enables Google My Business integration, and provides customers with immediate access to important business information like phone numbers and hours.

                


                

                    
How do I add schema markup to my website?

                    
Copy the generated JSON-LD code and paste it into the <head> section of your website's HTML. The schema markup will be automatically detected by search engines when they crawl your site. You can test it using Google's Rich Results Test.

                


                

                    
What business types are supported?

                    
Our tool supports all major business types including restaurants, retail stores, professional services, healthcare providers, automotive services, home services, legal services, financial services, and many more categories.

                


                

                    
Will this help my Google My Business listing?

                    
Yes, schema markup provides consistent business information across your website and Google My Business profile, which can improve your local search rankings and help Google better understand and display your business information.

                


                

                    
How often should I update my schema markup?

                    
Update your schema markup whenever your business information changes, such as address, phone number, opening hours, or business description. Keeping it current ensures search engines have accurate information to display.
