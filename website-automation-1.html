How it works

Three automations that work while you do.

Instant text follow-up

The moment someone enquires, they get a personalised text within 60 seconds — so they know you're on it.

"Hi John, thanks for your enquiry about electrical work. I'll call you within two hours. Urgent? Call 07xxx — Dave"

Missed-call text-back

Every missed call triggers an automatic text, so the customer feels acknowledged instead of moving on.

"Hi, sorry I missed your call — I'm on a job. Text me back or I'll call within the hour — Dave"

Automatic review requests

After a completed job, satisfied customers get a review request — building your reputation without you remembering to ask.

"Hi Sarah, hope you're happy with the work. A quick review would mean a lot: [link]"
